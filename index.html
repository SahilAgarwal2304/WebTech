Vivacé · Music

Date: 12–13 March 2026

Venue: School Auditorium

Description: Solo and ensemble performances across genres – classical, western, fusion. Prelims on 12th, finals on 13th.

Rules: Max 5 minutes per performance. Backing tracks allowed (submit by 10 March).

Coordinator: Ms. Sunita Rao

Abhinaya · Drama

Date: 13 March 2026

Venue: Open Air Theatre

Description: One-act plays and street theatre. Teams of 6–12.

Rules: 20–30 minutes. Props provided by school upon request.

Coordinator: Mr. Rajesh Khanna

Kalakriti · Visual Arts

Date: 12–14 March 2026

Venue: Art Gallery & Campus

Description: Live painting, sculpture, digital art, photography exhibition.

Rules: Submit entries by 10 March. Live painting on 12th.

Coordinator: Ms. Meera Desai
